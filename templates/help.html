Help

The Observing Portal is your access point to Las Cumbres Observatory's global network of telescopes. Registered users can:

  • Write and submit proposals and manage the membership of their research teams.
  • Plan, compose, and submit observing requests for approved proposals.
  • Track the status of submitted requests.
  • Identify and download data from completed observations.
  • Monitor the hours used by approved proposals.
  • Report problems, ask questions, and make suggestions.

If you are a new user of this Observing Portal, we strongly recommend that you read the "Getting Started on the LCO Global Telescope Network" Guide.

If you are a new user of the LCO network, information about the telescopes, instruments, data handling and quality, scheduling and other technical aspects of the network is available on the observatory capabilities pages.

Minimum Requirements

The following are the minimum versions of browsers required to use the observation portal. All other browsers/versions are supported on a best-effort basis.

  • Google Chrome/Chromium:48+
  • Mozilla Firefox: 46+
  • Apple Safari: 8+
  • Microsoft Edge: 14+
  • Internet Explorer: 9+ (minimal support)

If you require additional support, you may contact science support at science-support@lco.global. For inquiries about particular observation requests, please include the tracking and request numbers in your email.
