Below is a list of your active proposals in LCO's submission system. Submitted proposals are reviewed by LCO's Time Allocation Committee (TAC) whose members are selected from the astronomical community and are not affiliated with LCO. Proposals recommended for scheduling by the TAC are awarded time by LCO's director, and PIs are notified whether a proposal has been accepted approximately 3 weeks before the start of the semester.
